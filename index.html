01
Get Your API Keys
You need two keys: an Apify token from apify.com and a Claude API key from console.anthropic.com. Both have free tiers.
02
Discover Creators (AI)
Use the AI Creator Discovery panel to set your filters — platform, gender, follower range, country, and bio keywords. The AI will find matching profiles.
03
Send to Scanner
Select discovered profiles and click Send to Email Scanner — they auto-populate below. Or paste your own URLs directly.
04
Scan & Export
Click Scan Profiles. Each bio is checked for an email address. Click any email to copy it, or export all results as a CSV file.
AI Discovery uses Claude (Anthropic) to generate targeted hashtags and search terms, then Apify scrapes matching profiles and filters them against your criteria.
Note: Only emails publicly visible in a profile's bio will be detected. Country filtering uses bio text clues (e.g. "Sydney", "🇦🇺") as platforms don't always expose location data.
