01
Get Your API Keys
You need an Apify token from apify.com and a Claude API key from console.anthropic.com. Check "Remember keys" to save them in your browser.
02
Discover Creators (AI)
Use the AI Creator Discovery panel to set your filters. Claude will generate search hashtags, Apify scrapes profiles, and keywords are matched across bio, captions & hashtags.
03
Add to Scanner
Click Add on any discovered profile — it instantly appears in the URL box below. Or paste your own URLs directly.
04
Scan & Export
Click Scan Profiles. Filter results by found/not found. Copy all emails at once or export a CSV with handle, URL and email columns.
Scanning runs in parallel — up to 4 profiles at once for speed.
Emails detected: standard addresses plus obfuscated formats like [at], (dot), and emoji separators.
Session memory: your last scan results are saved in your browser and can be restored next visit.
